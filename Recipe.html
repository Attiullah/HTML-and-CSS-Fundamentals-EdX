Recipe 1

Today we will be making white rice. It is a common dish in south asia and in Central asian countries.
Get your armour ready becuase

"we will be feasting in hell tonight."

List of Ingredients:

  • Rice
  • water
  • salt
  • Oil
  • Onion
  • Zeera and other taste/color enhancers (Optional)

Steps:

  1. Soak the rice in water for 30 mins
  2. pour some oil in a pot
  3. Slice some onions
  4. Put the onions in oil and cook until turn brown
  5. Add water by measuring the correct ratio to rice
  6. Add salt as per taste
  7. Add taste enhancing components like zeera, etc
  8. Let the water boil
  9. Put the rice into the water and let it cook for 10 mins on medium to high flame.
  10. Lower the heat and cover the pot with something so no that the steam escapes. The longer the rice is kept like this, the better it'll taste.
  11. Take off of heat and enjoy with gravy.
